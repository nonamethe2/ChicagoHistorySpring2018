Following the expansion and economic growth of the mid nineteenth century the area prospered and for a time was known as the Astor Street District. Named after a New York businessman in an attempt to promote the image of wealth and success.
Pictured above is a street intersection at North Avenue and Astor Street in the early 1900s.
